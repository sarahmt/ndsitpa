About NeuroDining

__NeuroDining is providing a new experience for distinguished palates to explore New York City’s finest restaurants. Whether you have adventurous taste buds, a busy work schedule, clients to entertain or like most New Yorker’s, all of the above, NeuroDining ensures a dining experience that is certain to impress. As reliable customers, we make our diners visible to restaurants and eateries. We live by our list of selected restaurants and use member feedback to get the highest quality of personalization for our members. Our technology is designed so that you can focus on what truly matters: the satisfaction of sharing an exquisite meal with friends, family, or clients.

__In the age of Yelp and OpenTable, food quality and reliable reviews are often sacrificed for the sake of expediency. You have specific expectations that you cannot be certain other diners share. Worst of all, these services usually take a user considerable time to confirm reservations. By the time you go through endless and biased reviews, you face the headache of low availability and booked restaurants. NeuroDining members make reservations at New York City's top restaurants in less than one minute.
