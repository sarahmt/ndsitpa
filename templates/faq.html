FAQ

Reservations

How last minute can I make reservations?

Reservations can be confirmed up to 30 minutes prior to your reservation time.



Are reservations guaranteed when I see them as options?

Yes. The two restaurant suggestions appearing for your selected area are both guaranteed. All you have to do is click confirm. After 60 seconds, inventory/availability will be refreshed.



If I like a restaurant you suggested in the past, can I reserve a table through the ND APP?

Yes. You can access your past reservations under the Reservation History tab to easily re-book your favorite restaurants.



Why is the reservation name always under “ND member”?

We rely on the collective power of the NeuroDining community to maintain a unique customer status in the eyes of our affiliated establishments. Because the restaurant knows you are a NeuroDiner, you can expect the recognition and benefits associated with the membership.



Lateness & Cancellations

I am running late to my reservation. What do I do?

In general, you can expect your table to be held for 15 minutes past the reserved time. If you cannot arrive at the restaurant within this time frame, we suggest you contact the restaurant directly. Your reservation will always appear under “ND Member”.



How late can I cancel my reservation?

Cancelling your reservation is simple - just click on the cancel reservation button at the bottom of your screen. We ask that you indicate cancellations no later than 30 minutes prior to reservation time.



I could not show up to my reservation. Now what?

Since the NeuroDining community works hard to build reliable relationships between restaurants and members, we strongly suggest you avoid not showing up to a reservation without cancelling first. A lack of communication between the diner and restaurant may negatively impact the NeuroDining community as a whole. Of course, we understand that events beyond your control may sometimes cause you not to show up to your reservation. In this case, please select “I could not make it” when asked to review the restaurant on the iOS app.



Restaurant Suggestions

Will I keep getting the same restaurant suggestions over time?

Because NeuroDining maintains a list of top restaurants, this list is finite by nature. However, our list is constantly evolving to reflect openings, closings and user feedback.



I have a food restriction. Do you suggest restaurants based on that?

Currently, users are not able to filter suggestions based on food restrictions. However, you can easily access the restaurant’s menu through the iOS app to get a better sense of whether or not the restaurant will work for your food restrictions.



How are the two restaurant options chosen?

Restaurants are selected from our curated list of establishments that NeuroDining is confident will meet your expectations. We also tailor selections based on individual user feedback.



I do not wish to dine at either option. How long until I can get a new suggestion for the same area?

Sorry to hear that. In that case, let us know through feedback and we will do our best to make sure you do not encounter the same problem with future selections. Our inventory is refreshed every 60 seconds. Please visit the iOS app later to receive other options.



Feedback

What happens if I dont have a great experience at the restaurant ND suggested?

If you have a poor experience with one of our affiliated restaurants, we want to know! We strongly suggest sending feedback and sharing a review when asked to do so on the iOS app.



Why do I need to provide a review every time I dine out?

NeuroDining relies on user feedback to maintain the highest degree of excellence for our members. Your feedback will also be used to tailor future dining selections to suit your particular needs.



Account

I don't have a referral. Can I still join?

Currently, membership to NeuroDining is determined on a referral only basis. We are striving to maintain a consistent customer base made of reliable and respectable patrons as well as frequent diners and epicureans. If you believe you meet this description, please visit our “Request Membership” page to submit your contact information.



How can I update my email address, PW and referral code?

All changes to e-mail, password and referral code can be made on the account page of the iOS app.



How do I unsubscribe from the newsletters?

If you wish to unsubscribe from our newsletter, let us now at contact@neurodining.com and we will have you removed from our list as soon as possible.



How do I unsubscribe from the service?

If you wish to discontinue your NeuroDining membership, let us now at contact@neurodining.com. You will be refunded for any remaining value of your subscriptions.
