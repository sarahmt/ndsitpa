Our Story

__As a former banker and corporate executive, the founders of NeuroDining have firsthand experience with the frustrations of fine dining for busy professionals. From endless web pages with unreliable ratings to an ever changing landscape of eateries, searching for an exquisite meal is time consuming and risky. Frustrated by these obstacles and armed with adventurous taste buds, they set out to map the high end restaurants of New York City. Many delicious meals later, Neuroding was born.

__The founders now constantly work to maintain a list of restaurants that is carefully curated through extensive research and personal experience.
